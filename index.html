About Me

Hello, my name is Serafina Tolsche and I am a photographer based in Norway and New York City. I have formal background in photography from the Pratt Institute. I also received a Master from the New School at Parson in marketing. I specialize in fashion photography and have previously worked with several notable brands. In addition, my background from Parson enable me to fully conceptualize and market your fashion line.
